The Power of Promise

Dear Partners,

The last year has laid bare how inequity continues to shape the environments where Chicagoland children grow up. These realities show up every day—in classrooms, in communities, and in futures constrained too early. And still, our young people remain hopeful. They have not given up on their futures, and neither can we.

This moment calls for leadership that acts early, invests wisely, and listens closely to community voice—because the conditions shaping young people’s lives today determine the outcomes we see tomorrow. Together with our cohort of community-based organizations and a diverse network of partners and experts, Bright Promises continues to evolve to respond to urgent needs while building toward long-term change.

In the pages that follow, The Power of Promise explores the critical space between environments and outcomes—the “missing middle” where development happens. This report shows what becomes possible when communities, partners, and donors come together to build the conditions young people need to thrive.

As you read, we invite you to see how acting early—together—protects opportunity and builds the future our children deserve.

In Solidarity,Katherine KoreyExecutive Director
Bright Promises Foundation
Key Insight #1

Children’s access to opportunity is increasingly shaped by forces beyond their control, and the opportunity gap is widening.

Economic uncertainty, policy shifts, attacks on equity-focused work, and growing stress on public and nonprofit systems are reshaping daily life for families across Chicagoland. These conditions are compounding and widening opportunity gaps for children.

Key Insight #1

Organizations are being asked to do more with dramatically less.1

Rising stress, trauma, and fractured systems have increased demand for their services—just as funding has become more volatile and uncertain.

Bright Promises is responding alongside our partners—building on trust, shared leadership, and local solutions—to ensure children and families have the support, tools, and relationships they need to navigate this moment and emerge stronger.

Bright Promises’ model of collective giving is helping stabilize these trusted community anchors—so they can keep showing up when families need them most.

Key Insight #4

Effective solutions start early.

Bright Promises’ model proves that early intervention and coordination lead to better outcomes at lower cost. By strengthening organizations, aligning systems, and addressing challenges before they escalate into crisis, Bright Promises turns early action into lasting impact for children, families, and communities.

Key Insight #4

Early investment. Stronger systems. Lasting impact.

Bright Promises drives results by acting early—before needs escalate and costs multiply. Our upstream initiatives targeting early childhood education, youth mental health, and youth leadership and civic engagement move effective ideas out of silos and into practice, shaping how systems respond to children and families over time.

What Bright Promises Upstream Initiatives Produce:

  • Proven practices coordinated across systems, not just piloted in individual programs.
  • Durable change that continues beyond the life of a grant.
  • Replication across organizations and sectors, expanding reach without increasing cost.
  • Reduced reliance on costly downstream interventions by preventing crisis before it occurs.

Scaling what works through trust, partnership, and community leadership creates lasting opportunity for children.

Key Insight #5

Bright Promises helps community-based organizations scale what works while staying responsive to the people they serve.

Bright Promises combines the risk-tolerance of venture philanthropy with the relationship-building and movement power of grassroots organizing.

Bright Promises’ cohort of community partners represent a variety of organization sizes, with operating budgets ranging from under $500,000 to over $10 million. Despite their differences in size or geography, leaders from these organizations unanimously agree that Bright Promises connects them with a community that provides a safe, positive learning environment.

The result is a stronger, more coordinated ecosystem where effective practices spread, organizations grow together, and children benefit from opportunity that lasts.

Looking Ahead: Our Goals

Embed trauma-informed and SEL practices

Into organizational policy, leadership, and daily practice across community-based organizations throughout Chicagoland—so care, safety, and belonging are built into the systems children experience every day.

Strengthen workforce stability and resilience

By reducing burnout, supporting retention, and investing in the people who carry this work forward, even under extraordinary strain.

Build shared infrastructure for scale and influence

Enabling tools, learning, and promising practices to spread beyond individual grantees and drive broader systems uptake.

These goals reflect what our community has told us matters most—now and for the future.

If you believe children deserve more—and that communities already hold the answers—we invite you to keep building this future with us.

Corporate Partner Spotlight

West Monroe’s long-standing partnership with Bright Promises goes beyond volunteerism.

In the communities we serve, increased ICE activity has intensified fear and uncertainty—causing anxiety, trauma, and chronic stress. Mental health kits assembled by West Monroe volunteers are a critical part of Bright Promises’ response. Distributed through trusted community partners, these kits connect families to free, trauma-informed SEL resources through the SEL Access Exchange.

With demand far exceeding staff capacity, partnerships like this are essential—enabling Bright Promises to meet urgent need and respond with care during moments of crisis.

Our Mission

Bright Promises Foundation’s mission is to identify, fund, and share solutions to underrecognized needs of children and youth most adversely impacted by inequity.

Our Vision

Our vision is for a Chicagoland where all children and youth reach their full promise and potential.
